Where Am I — The Motorcycle Rider's Companion 🏍️

Everything you need on the road, in one clean app. Where Am I is built for motorcyclists who want live local information, smart trip logging, and peace of mind in emergencies — with no account, no ads, and no data collection.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🧭 RIDER HQ
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Your live dashboard while riding. See your current address, live weather with hourly and 3-day forecasts, a riding-suitability score with practical recommendations, sunrise and sunset times, and road-condition alerts — all from your current GPS position.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🍽️ FOOD STOPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Find restaurants, cafés, bars, bakeries and fast food nearby, sorted by distance. Tap any result for directions, contact info, or their website.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🛏️ CRASH PADS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Locate hotels, hostels, campsites and other places to sleep near your current position — perfect when the day's ride runs long.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🏁 ROADSIDE STOPS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Discover viewpoints, museums, landmarks and other stops worth pulling over for along your route.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🔧 THE GARAGE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Everything to keep you riding:
• Motorcycle dealers, repair workshops & parts shops
• Fuel stations (with fuel type info)
• Parking — general and motorcycle-specific
• MC clubs & racing tracks
• ATMs & banks

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
📏 TRIP LOGGER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Record your rides with a live speed gauge, real-time distance and duration counters, and GPS route tracking that keeps working with the screen locked. Your full ride history is saved on your device — never uploaded anywhere.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🆘 SOS / EMERGENCY
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
A dedicated emergency screen, one tap away:
• One-tap call to the local emergency number
• Universal emergency numbers (112, 911, 999, 000, 111)
• Nearest hospitals, police, fire stations & pharmacies
• Location sharing via SMS or WhatsApp
• Torch / flash screen for signalling
• Step-by-step first aid for motorcycle accidents

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
🌍 9 LANGUAGES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Full interface in English, Spanish, German, French, Icelandic, Norwegian, Swedish, Danish and Dutch — switch instantly, remembered forever.

🔒 PRIVACY — NO DATA COLLECTED
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

• No ads, analytics or tracking
• Your location is used only on-device
